Our Mission

We're a grassroots movement committed to cleaning up litter and debris from roadsides to make neighborhoods safer, cleaner, and more beautiful. We empower volunteers and local leaders to take action and protect the environment.

How It Works

Step 1: Choose a cleanup day and location.
Step 2: We provide gear and safety tips.
Step 3: Gather your group and clean!
Step 4: Share your results and inspire others.
